WHAT IS A CBT TEST?

A Computer Based Test, otherwise called CBT is an exam that is taken with the use of a computer. The exam conducted by the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board, JAMB in 2013 introduced this system and since then it has been in use. From 2024, the WASSCE conducted by the West African Examination Council, WAEC will be Hybrid exam. Hybrid exam is a combination of CBT and Written questions.

EDUSUCESS CBT TESTS

Edusucess tries to help students familiarize themselves with likely questions and formats they may find in real-time examinations. To achieve this, there will be CBT held at pre-informed dates via the Edusucess app. Each CBT test can only be answered once per student and the results is shown immediately after you submit.
