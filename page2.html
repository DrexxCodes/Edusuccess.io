Getting Started and setting up the app

INSTALLATION

To get started you need to install the Edusucess App. The app is ANDROID ONLY and to get the app, all you need to do is click the "Get App" button at the top of the screen and the download begins automatically. Once completed, select the downloaded app and install.

CREATING YOUR ACCOUNT

After installing the the app, you need to make an account. To make an account on the app, simply fill out the required fields and pay attention to your password. After you submit the registration page initially, we shall verify your email address and once that is done, you would enter your password again and await validation. To prevent instances of double accounts belonging to one person, you need to be validated before your account is activated. All students are required to fill a form that would be used to create their portals and the email supplied there should be the email used to create an account with us.

ACCOUNT VALIDATION

To make use of the Edusuccess App, you need to verify your account. To verify your account, you have to send your email to our affiliate Telegram Bot. Here's how to get it done. 

STEP 1: Get the Bot here

STEP 2: Select Edusucess App Validation option on the bot and enter your Username you used to open the account.

STEP 3: After sending your Username, you will be contacted via the bot with a confirmation of the account validation. NOTE: DO NOT INTERACT DIRECTLY WITH THE BOT WITH GREETINGS

Lastly you should get this notification from EduSuccess mobile. For any problems or questions you need during the installation, initiate a live chat with us and we shall attend to you.

NECESSITIES & DEPENDENCIES

The App makes use of two broadcast units. A one way unit and a double way unit. The one way unit is a notification channel and it is automatically created when the app is installed on your device. This enables us to send notifications to you where need be. The double way unit has to be enabled by you. All you have to do is send "Hi" to the double way chat unit DM.

How long does account validation takes?

For the bot to verify your account, it takes an average of 15 minutes with all extra factors such as Server Speed and Database cloud fare considered. If it takes longer to get a reply send a Whatsapp Message to Drexx

How safe is the App?

We have data migration policies in place to protect your data from fishing. Our servers are highly encrypted and regularly checked for inconsistencies, so the app is as safe as it can be.

Does Edusuccess sell my data?

No, neither Edusuccess nor its affiliates can or will sell your personal data. We may however use certain demographic data and raised questions for profiling and newsletters or any program by our third parties that is carried out within the organization
